# Data Documentation:

## Natality Dataset

# Numeric Columns:
- **mager**: Mother's Age (yrs)
- **precare**: Month that prenatal care began
- **bmi**: Mother's Pre-Pregnancy BMI
- **apgar5**: 5-Minute APGAR score (1-10)
- **dplural**: Plurality of birth (2=twins, etc.)
- **dbwt**: Birthweight


# Categorical Columns:
- **bfacil**: Birth Facility Type (6 Categories)
    - Hospital
    - Home (intended)
    - Home (not intended)
    - Freestanding Birth Center
    - Clinic / Doctor's Office
    - Other
- **mrace6**: Mother's Race (6 categories)
    - White
    - Asian
    - Black
    - American Indian or Alaska Native
    - Native Hawaiian and Other Pacific Islander
    - More than one race
- **dmar**: Mother's Marital Status (Married/Unmarried)
- **meduc**: Mother's Education Level (8 categories)
    - 8th grade or less
    - 9th-12th no diploma
    - High school graduate or GED completed
    - Some college credit
    - Associate Degree
    - Bachelor's Degree
    - Master's Degree
    - Doctorate
- **me_rout**: Delivery Routine (4 Categories)
    - Spontaneous
    - Cesarean
    - Vacuum 
    - Forceps

# Boolean Columns:
- **rf_pdiab**: Pre-Pregnancy Diabetes
- **rf_gdiab**: Gestastional Diabetes
- **rf_phype**: Pre-Pregnancy Hypertension
- **rf_ghype**: Gestational Hypertension
- **rf_ehype**: Eclampsia
- **no_risks**: None of the above risks
- **ip_gon**: Gonorrhea Infection during Pregnancy
- **ip_syph**: Syphilis Infection during Pregnancy
- **ip_chlam**: Chlamydia Infection during Pregnancy
- **ip_hepb**: Hepatitis B Infection during Pregnancy
- **ip_hepc**: Hepatitis C Infection during Pregnancy
- **no_infec**: None of the above infections during Pregnancy
- **ld_indl**: Labor was Induced
- **ld_augm**: Labor was Augmented
- **ld_ster**: Steroids given during Labor
- **ld_antb**: Antibiotics given during Labor
- **ld_chor**: Chorioamnionitis diagnosed during Labor
- **ld_anes**: Anesthesia used during Labor
- **mm_mtr**: Maternal Transfusion 
- **mm_plac**: Placental Abruption 
- **mm_rupt**: Uterine Rupture
- **mm_uhyst**: Unplanned Hysterectomy
- **mm_aicu**: Maternal ICU Admission
- **ab_aven1**: Assisted Ventilation Required < 6hrs
- **ab_aven6**: Assisted Ventilation Required > 6 hrs
- **ab_nicu**: Infant Admitted to NICU
- **ab_surf**: Surfactant Therapy 
- **ab_anti**: Antibiotics given to Infant
- **ab_seiz**: Seizure Activity in Infant
- **no_abnorm**: No Abnormal Conditions in Infant
